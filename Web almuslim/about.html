About Us

HISTORY

The Almuslim Peusangan Foundation in Bireuen, Aceh Province was established on 21 Jumadil Akhir 1348 H, corresponding to November 24, 1929. Initially named Jami'ah Al Muslim, its founding figures included Tgk. Abdurrahman Meunasah Meucap, Tgk. Chik Muhammad Djohan Alamsyah (Ulee Balang Peusangan), Tgk. H. Ibrahim Meunasah Barat, Tgk. Abbas Bardan, Tgk. Abed Idham, Habib Muhammad, Tgk. Ridwan, and others.

The establishment of Almuslim Peusangan in 1929 took place amidst the war between the people of Aceh and Dutch soldiers, in a struggle for independence led by Tgk. Abdurrahman Meunasah Meucap. During this prolonged conflict, they observed and realized a dwindling optimism for achieving victory due to changing circumstances and conditions, which in turn led to tactics and strategies that were only advantageous to the enemy. Because the enemy was fighting far from their homeland, they could think calmly in their own country while consolidating themselves by acquiring new sources of strength, both in terms of knowledge and products, to deploy on the battlefield. This situation was in stark contrast to the conditions of the Acehnese fighters, who at that time were thinking and preparing various strategies while simultaneously engaged in war. As a result, everything was neglected, including the economy, state life, and education. In such a situation, the freedom fighter Tgk. Abdurrahman Meunasah Meucap and his companions concluded that all of this was due to ignorance. Therefore, they sought another way, namely to build and light a lamp in the darkness, to illuminate the path they would take to address this challenge by establishing the Almuslim Peusangan Education or Foundation with a curriculum tailored to the needs of that time, using an integrated and comprehensive system. This was a comprehensive curriculum, so that its graduates at that time became experts in various disciplines.

Almuslim University in Peusangan, Bireuen, Aceh Province, is the only university in Bireuen Regency. Established based on the Decree of the Minister of National Education of the Republic of Indonesia Number: 02/D/O/2003, it was a transformation from several higher education institutions within the Almuslim Higher Education Institution, namely the Agricultural College, the Teacher Training and Education College, and the Academy of Management and Computer Science, which were founded on 14 Dzulqa'dah 1406 H or August 1, 1985. At the age of 12, Almuslim University had 8,149 active students, had graduated 18,239 graduates, had 517 lecturers, most of whom held Master's and Doctoral degrees, and 250 administrative and supporting staff. Currently, there are also 73 lecturers pursuing their Master's and Doctoral studies at various universities both domestically and internationally with scholarships from the Higher Education Funding Agency of the Ministry of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia and scholarships from Almuslim University."

UNIVERSITY STRUCTURE

ACHIEVEMENTS

As a leading private university in Aceh, Almuslim University has demonstrated a strong commitment to advancing higher education in the region. Since its establishment in 1985, Umuslim has successfully produced thousands of high-quality graduates who have contributed to the nation's development. The repeated recognition as the Best Private University in Aceh is a testament to its quality and dedication to providing excellent education.
